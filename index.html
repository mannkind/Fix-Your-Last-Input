Requirements

Adium

You can checkout and compile Adium to get the required frameworks See http://trac.adiumx.com/wiki/GettingReleaseAdiumSource for more info on checking out the Adium source.

  • Adium.framework
  • AIUtilities.framework
  • Fribidi.framework

libpcre

You can download and compile libpcre yourself, or you can get it from the ObjPCRE SourceForge files

  • http://sourceforge.net/projects/objpcre/files/
